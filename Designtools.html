Graphic design, video editing, and presentation tools

design in your way

Canva

Easy-to-use graphic design tool with drag-and-drop functionality, offers templates for social media graphics, posters, and presentations.

GIMP

Open-source image editor with advanced features similar to Photoshop, supports layers, filters, and plugins.

Inkscape

Free vector graphics editor, ideal for creating logos, illustrations, and scalable designs.

Krita

Open-source painting and illustration tool, supports professional drawing tablets and offers various brushes and effects.

Blender

Comprehensive 3D creation suite, supports modeling, animation, rendering, and video editing.

DaVinci Resolve

Professional video editing software, offers color correction, visual effects, and audio post-production tools.

Shotcut

Free, open-source video editor, supports a wide range of formats and offers a variety of video and audio editing features.

Prezi

Dynamic presentation tool, allows for non-linear presentations with zoomable canvas, ideal for storytelling.

Google Slides

Free presentation tool within Google Workspace, offers real-time collaboration and a variety of templates.

Lumen5

AI-powered video creation tool, converts text content into engaging videos, suitable for marketing and social media.
